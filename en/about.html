About

I am an interdisciplinary designer exploring the intersection of built environment, technology, and interaction. My work integrates architectural thinking with digital and computational processes, approaching design through a spatial, environmental, and systemic perspective.

With a background in architecture and master’s degrees in interactive design and creative computing, I transitioned from shaping physical environments to creating hybrid experiences that blend physical and digital interactions. My projects span digital transformation, healthcare, subscription platforms, IoT-driven interaction, and blockchain-enabled storytelling—translating complexity into clarity through design.

I am particularly interested in how nature and technology can inspire more intuitive and meaningful ways of living and interacting. This perspective grounds my design philosophy and guides my exploration of data-driven narratives, decentralized interactions, and emerging forms of digital culture.
