Designing hybrid systems across environments, services, and technology

I design hybrid systems that translate environmental, social, and organizational processes into meaningful human experiences. Working at the intersection of research, strategy, and systems design, my practice focuses on structuring interaction models, workflows, and platform experiences that support long-term engagement and understanding.

Grounded in spatial and environmental thinking, I help teams design physical–digital ecosystems with clarity and intention, making relationships between people, technology, and environments visible, actionable, and trustworthy.

This is a curated selection of my work, focused on experience and systems design across digital, spatial, and hybrid contexts.
