nky-portfolio | Design Rationale v1.0 (October 2025)
============================================================
CONCEPT
------------------------------------------------------------
The NKY Portfolio blends minimalism with digital elegance,
reflecting the intersection of architecture, design, and technology.
The visual identity is neutral and structured, with subtle motion
and a refined purple accent representing creativity and innovation.

------------------------------------------------------------
VISUAL SYSTEM
------------------------------------------------------------
- Background → pure white (#ffffff) for neutrality.
- Accent color → elegant purple (#b84be0) for contrast and recognition.
- Font → Poppins (Google Fonts), weights 300–700.
- Layout → Grid system with 3–2–1 responsive columns.
- Max width → 960 px for optimal balance and readability.

------------------------------------------------------------
BEHAVIOR
------------------------------------------------------------
- Hover overlay in desktop adds depth with dark transparency.
- Mobile version prioritizes clarity: text below the image.
- Sticky navbar for intuitive navigation.
- Footer anchored to bottom even when content is short.

------------------------------------------------------------
TONE & STYLE
------------------------------------------------------------
The tone is professional, calm, and contemporary.
Typography weights replace color contrasts to define hierarchy.
Grayscale palette supports neutrality and timelessness.
