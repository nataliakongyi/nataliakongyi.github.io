Selected Systems & Product Works

I am a Product Lead & Systems Designer with a 15-year foundation in architectural systems and over 8 years specializing in digital and hybrid product development. I excel at 0-to-1 environments—taking fragmented institutional or technical challenges and turning them into coherent, scalable digital ecosystems.

My approach merges an architectural systemic approach with functional prototyping. Whether designing for social impact in international organizations or building IoT-driven platforms, I lead the entire lifecycle: framing ambiguity, aligning stakeholders, and validating hardware-software interfaces through real-world user testing.
