Exploring hybrid realities through space, technology, and digital experiences.

Interdisciplinary Designer & Researcher


I design and study interactions across spatial, digital, and emerging technological environments. My work bridges service design, UX research, interactive media, and creative technologies to explore how people engage with systems. I investigate how digital interfaces, immersive environments, and interactive narratives can reshape the ways we inhabit space— weaving together the physical and digital dimensions of experience.
